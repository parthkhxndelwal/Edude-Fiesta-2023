About

EDUDE FIESTA’23 is an annual culmination of various Cultural, Techno and Sports Competitions, lasting 2 days, from 24rd to 25th November 2023. The uniqueness of this event lies in the diversityit witnesses in terms of student participation from various universities, colleges, and institutionsacross India. The event provides opportunities as well as alluring experiences to all the participants,to interact with fellow students, experts, notable personalities and also gain inspiration from them.Besides, there will be an opportunity for best-performing University or College to win Trophiesand titles:

About KR Mangalam University

We foster a culture of innovation and intellectual exploration, empowering students to generate fresh ideas and embark on a path of profound learning and personal growth. Our approach to education is multidisciplinary, ensuring a comprehensive and balanced educational experience. We are the most credible University in Gurgaon that puts great emphasis on practical knowledge, industry exposure, and research-driven learning methodologies.

Sports Events

Cricket

24 & 25 Nov 2023, Full Day

Team Sport

Cricket involves two teams, each consisting of 11 players, taking turns to bat and field. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and defending wickets. The team scoring more runs in specified over wins.

Volleyball (Men)

24 & 25 Nov 2023, Full Day

Team Sport

Volleyball involves two teams, each with six players, aim to score points by sending a ball over the net and into the opposing team's court, while preventing it from touching their own side. Players use a combination of spiking, bumping, and setting to strategically maneuver the ball. It's a fast-paced, thrilling game that demands teamwork, agility, and precise skills.

Badminton (Men)

24 & 25 November 2023, Full Day

Solo

Badminton is a fast-paced racquet sport played indoors or outdoors by two or four players. The objective is to score points by hitting a shuttlecock over a net and into the opponent's side of the court. It requires agility, precision, and quick reflexes, making it an exciting and challenging sport.

Table Tennis (Men)

24 & 25 November 2023, Full Day

Solo/Duet

Table tennis, also known as ping pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played on a rectangular table with a net across the middle. Players use small paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth, aiming to score points by getting the ball to bounce on the opponent's side of the table.

Tennis

24 & 25 November 2023, Full Day

Solo/Duet

Tennis is a fast-paced, competitive racket sport played by two individuals (singles) or four (doubles) on a rectangular court divided by a net. Players aim to score points by hitting a ball over the net, making it land within the opponent's court.

Fitness Pro Challenge 3.0

25-Nov-23, Full Day

Solo

The Fitness Pro challenge will provide students with an insight about their current fitness level and further motivate them to indulge in a healthy lifestyle. This particular event will include gross testing of cardiovascular fitness, agility, and strength. An obstacle course will be created keeping different fitness parameters in mind. Total time taken will be noted and would decide the winners.
